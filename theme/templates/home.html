{% trans 'How the purchasing group works' %}

{% trans 'Members of the group voluntarily collaborate and coordinate on key tasks each week to bring affordable food to the community' %}

  1. 1

    {% trans 'Make a grocery wish list for different baskets of goods with a set budget' %}

    {% trans 'Monday' %}-{% trans 'Tuesday' %}

    {% trans 'From Monday to Tuesday, volunteers select food items beforehand according to availability and the group\'s tastes. Each selected item is assigned to some basket of goods with a predefined budget. As many oranges and bananas might be selected to fill a budget of $10 for the Fruit basket.' %}
  2. 2

    {% trans 'Members choose among baskets of goods' %}

    {% trans 'Tuesday' %}-{% trans 'Thursday' %}

    {% trans 'From Tuesday to Thursday, members are invited to select from the preconstructed baskets, and in quantities that suit their needs.' %}
  3. 3

    {% trans 'Translate member orders into a purchase list at the distributor' %}

    {% trans 'Thursday' %}

    {% trans 'Compiling all the member order requests into an actionable grocery list at the distributor is tricky! Prices and availability of items fluctuate daily, and are sold in quantities and units not found at the average retail grocery store. A few volunteers will take on the task of travelling to the distributor in order to verify the latest prices and stock, and then adjust the purchase list as a function of the budget set by the weekly member order requests.' %}
  4. 4

    {% trans 'Purchase the group\'s order at the distributor' %}

    {% trans 'Friday' %}

    {% trans 'With the group\'s order in hand, volunteers will travel directly to the distributor and purchase all the desired items, possibly replacing one item for another to account for unpredictabilities, but always staying within the budget!' %}
  5. 5

    {% trans 'Prepare and distribute member baskets at the pick-up point' %}

    {% trans 'Friday' %}

    {% trans 'Once the volunteers transport the order at the distributor back to the Espace des Possibles, the food items are portioned into as many individual baskets as there were requests from members that week. Members of the collective stop by the Espace des Possible at one of two available time slots to collect their order.' %}
